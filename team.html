Meet the Team

We’re a close-knit team that truly cares about making great work that advances the greater good of our partners’ mission. Our team of in-house creative, media, technology, and strategy experts is always collaborating and sharing ideas to tackle challenges and create engaging solutions for each and every project we touch.

Team

Richard O Mangala
CEO - Founder
Richard Mangala founded Mangala Design Group in 2014, which later was rebranded to Echo Marketing Branding Company to express the evolved capabilities of the agency. Now, as Echo is a full-service marketing and communications firm, Richard continues to be a leading force for our team and provides direction for the agency. He also maintains several long-time client relationships that contribute to our growing portfolio. In addition to the value and creativity he brings to our partners’ brands, Richard also provides insight and guidance that he has generated throughout the years, ensuring the team delivers the proven strategy and processes that have made Echo successful since its early beginnings.
